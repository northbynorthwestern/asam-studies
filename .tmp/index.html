Gameday

Inside the anarchy of a Big Ten football game

Football games have a different meaning, depending on who you ask. Students see them as social events, players see them as the highlight of their weeks and staff members see them as their job.

On November 8, Michigan visited Evanston to take on the Wildcats in a battle of two below-average teams. The outcome wasn't pretty — a 10-9 Wolverine victory — but what happened behind the scenes throughout the game was a work of art. Chaos was successfully controlled, and logistically, everything went to plan. Normally, when everything goes to plan, that isn't newsworthy. But hey, this isn't normal. It's Gameday.
